How to make a pizza

Ever wanted to make a pizza? youve come to the right place, heres what you need...

Dough

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 standard packet
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

sauce and toppings

  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of freshly shreded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Your desired choice of toppings

Now that you have everything you need here are the steps

  1. the yeast: In a large bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.
  2. Form the dough: Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix until a sticky, shaggy dough begins to form.
  3. Knead: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 to 7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Let it rise: Place the dough into a lightly oiled bowl. Cover with a warm, damp towel or plastic wrap and let it rise for 45 to 60 minutes, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat: Preheat your oven to (475^F) If you are using a baking stone or inverted baking sheet, leave it in the oven to get hot.
  6. Shape the dough: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Add toppings: Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper or a pizza peel. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle the mozzarella, oregano, and your chosen toppings on top.
  8. Bake: Bake on your hot baking sheet or pizza stone for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Serve: Let the pizza cool for 2 to 3 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy!!
